Studio Soul

Studio Soul is a multi-functional, open workplace that specializes in promoting well-being, health, and good lifestyle. It takes pride in fostering an environment where everyone is welcome and inspired. Employees work in a space that is specifically designed to cater towards their needs, enabling them to put their best work forward in growing their respective brand. Produced in AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Enscape, my task involved selecting finishes, furniture, and space planning to create a well-functioning business.
