Pulse

Pulse is a healthcare unit modeled in SketchUp for the purpose of furthering healthcare design skills and building knowledge for compliancy with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). The inspiration for the design is focused on using simple yet effective design elements to create a calm and soothing environment for patients.
