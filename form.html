Form

Create a light fixture that will be used on the outdoor patio of a restaurant of choice. Form uses organic, sustainable materials with a touch of green to add freshness throughout the space. The fixture will be made out of a green ceramic glass base with a brass holder/tubing. It also includes LED lighting to maximize efficiency and save money. With a long life duration, Form also produces warmer tones with improved color which will enhance the green ceramic glass. Glass is a sustainable, fully recyclable material which provides great environmental benefits. This project has been created using SketchUp and Enscape.

Brass

Ceramic Glass
